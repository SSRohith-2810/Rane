RETURN AND EXCHANGE PROCEDURE :

To initiate an exchange, please visit our contact page in our official RANE website or make an exchange request mail at raneservice@gmail.com within 7 days of receiving the item we will write back to you within 24 hours.



QUALITY CHECK :

All exchanges will undergo a quality check by sending us the images of the product you received via mail. The item will be decline an exchange if:

1. The product/products are returned after use.
2. The product/products are damaged.


SHIPPING THE EXCHANGE :

If exchange is confirmed, the customer is resposible for shipping the product back to RANE. Please provide the tracking ID at raneservice@gmail.com in the same email thread.


PROCESSING TIME :

Once we receive the returned item, we will process the exchange within 5-7 bussiness days.


DEFECTIVE PRODUCTS :

If you received a defective product, please share us the order details and images of the product at raneservice@gmail.com and we will respond within 24 hours.


EXCHANGE LIMITATIONS:

Exchanges are allowed for the same product for size only once. Exchanges are subjective to avalibility. if the desired size is not in stock, it will take more time to exchange and dispatch the product.


PRICE CHANGES:

RANE reserves the right to change the prices of the products we are selling. The prices of the products will, during the time of placing an order, be valid throughout that specific process.



SHIPPING POLICY :


STANDARD DELIVERY:

1. Orders will be shipped as soon as after the manufacturing is done.
2. Orders will be delivered within 6-7 Business days, not inclusive of public holidays and manufacturing period.
3. Delivery can take 7-9 Business days as well because of the COVID situation.


PRODUCTION ON DEMAND (POD):

1. We start to manufacture the item as soon as we receive your order.
